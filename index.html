Shmoot's Various Projects



  • PovChem: Create animated POV-Ray scenes from your Spacechem solutions. Mostly automatically.
  • Fallout Password Cracker: Computer terminals in the 3D-era Fallout games all seem to share a certain fatal security vulnerability. How very careless of the manufacturer. This tool will help you exploit that vulnerability.
  • 'Nother Powerful Nifty Purchase: A few levels into Skyrim you'll be hauling back more loot than a merchant can buy at once, but if you don't sell them exactly as much in loot as they have in gold then you're not getting as good a deal as you should be: If they have any gold left at the end then you're leaving them some gold that you should have, and if you sell them something for less than it is worth to get that gold then you'll eventually leave yourself with less gold than you should have. This tool will find a subset of your loot that exactly matches the merchant's available gold, if such a subset exists. Possibly also useful elsewhere. It's the subset sum problem.
  • Skyrim Alchemy Brew Lab: I'm often running off to UESP to remind myself of the best recipe for a particular potion, or to see how many extra effects I can cram in. So I wrote this. I'm sure they'll appreciate the bandwidth savings.
  • Don't Starve Together Calendar. Because why would I make a much more convenient in-game mod?
